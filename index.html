Seamless Fun

More Fluid. More Secure. More Fun.

realme UI 3.0 introduces a refreshing new look with Fluid Space design and Sketchpad AOD, new features to help

protect your privacy, and improvements across the entire system to make your realme smartphone faster and more

fluid than ever. There is so much to say about realme UI 3.0 - here are just a few of our favorite features.

Fluid Space Design

Delightful Icons Through and Through

The new 3D icon uses layers and colors to provide a sense of context and visibility. Every icon is redrawn

and redesigned. Unnecessary strokes and shadows have been removed. Each 3D icon sticks to a precise

color palette to create more harmonious relationships between each element, resulting in a distinctive and

pleasing icon design.

Personalization now gets

even more Personal

Smart Theming Engine

The new Smart Theming Engine automatically picks colors from your wallpaper and applies those colors

across system elements of realme UI 3.0. So your interface UI now feels more personal and cohesive across

the board.

Truly Yours with Sketchpad AOD

The all-new Sketchpad AOD makes the lock screen experience personal. Sketchpad AOD converts your

portrait wallpaper into artistic AOD aesthetics that are indeed yours. So whenever you unlock your phone,

Sketchpad AOD fades smoothly into your home screen.

Seamlessly Fluid Experience

Based on the latest Android 12, realme UI 3.0 is built upon new technologies and features to give you

enhanced privacy controls, faster system performance, more personalized setting options, and more.*

AI Smooth Engine

realme UI 3.0 now has a redesigned resources management system with AI Smooth Engine, making more

efficient use of CPU and GPU to make system performance smoother, faster launch apps, and improves

battery life. So everything you do now on your realme smartphone feels more responsive and fluid.

Because realme UI 3.0 utilizes Smooth Animation Engine, every interaction on realme smartphone now
